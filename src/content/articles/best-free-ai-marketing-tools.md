## What "Free" Actually Means in 2026

There are three types of "free" AI writing tools:

1. **Truly free** — unlimited use, no credit card, no time limit (rare)
2. **Free tier** — limited words/credits per month, resets monthly
3. **Free trial** — time-limited access to paid features, then paywall

We'll flag each type clearly so you know what you're getting.

## What Free Tools Can't Do Well

It's worth being honest about the limitations of free AI writing tools:

**SEO optimisation:** Free tools don't tell you which keywords to include, what heading structure to use, or how your content compares to ranking pages. For that, you need Frase or Surfer SEO.

**Brand voice consistency:** Free tools don't learn your tone of voice. Every piece needs manual editing to sound like you.

**Factual accuracy:** All AI tools hallucinate. Free tiers don't include real-time web access, so any statistics or recent facts need verification.

**Scale:** If you're producing 20+ pieces of content per month, free limits become a bottleneck quickly.
