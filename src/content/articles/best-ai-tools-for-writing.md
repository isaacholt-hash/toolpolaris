## How to Get the Most from AI Writing Tools

The writers getting the best results from AI tools share three habits:

**1. Give the AI a detailed brief.** "Write a blog post about email marketing" produces generic output. "Write a 1,500-word blog post for marketing managers at SaaS companies about improving email open rates, focusing on subject line strategies. Tone: direct, practical, no fluff" produces something usable.

**2. Edit for specificity.** AI writing tends toward the generic and hedged. The editing pass should add concrete examples, real numbers, specific recommendations, and your actual opinion.

**3. Use AI for structure, humans for insight.** AI is excellent at producing structured first drafts. The parts that make content genuinely useful — original analysis, counterintuitive insights, authentic voice — still come from humans.
