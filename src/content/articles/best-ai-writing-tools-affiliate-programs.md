## How to Actually Make Money With These Programs

Most affiliate marketers fail not because the programs are bad, but because they promote tools without a traffic strategy. Here's what works:

**1. Write comparison articles.** "Jasper vs. Writesonic vs. Copy.ai" outranks individual reviews and captures readers already in buying mode. These convert at 3–5x the rate of general "what is AI writing" content.

**2. Build a 'Best for...' structure.** Instead of one winner, tell readers which tool is best for their specific situation. Readers self-select into the right tool, and you earn commission regardless of which they pick.

**3. Target the right keyword intent.** "AI writing tools affiliate programs 2026" (what you just searched) is a marketer doing research. "Best AI writing tool for blog posts" is someone about to buy. Both are valuable — but target the second type first.

**4. Use long cookie programs for SEO content.** Semrush's 120-day and HubSpot's 90-day cookie windows mean your articles keep earning commissions months after they rank.

**5. Match the tool to your audience's price sensitivity.** Frase at $15/month converts better for new bloggers than Surfer at $89/month, even though Surfer pays more per sale.
